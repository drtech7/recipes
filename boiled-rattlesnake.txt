Ingredients:
 - 1 rattlesnake with rattles removed
 - 2 cups sugar
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 1 cup milk
 - 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
 - 1 cup boiling water

Directions:
 1. In a large bowl, combine ingredients and mix until smooth.  Stir in
    boiling water (batter will be thin). 
 
 2. Boil 30 to 35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out
    clean. Cool completely.

 3. Throw in trash.
